Exams

Digital exams


  • You have max. three attempts for each exam.
  • You are automatically registered for exams and reexams.
  • If you do not show for an exam or a reexam, you will use one exam attempt.
  • If you fall sick on the day of an exam, you must submit a medical ertificate within four days to the study administration to not use an exam attempt.
  • You will be assessed on an individual basis, also when the exam is based on a group project.
  • Plagiarism and cheating on an exam is taken very seriously.
  • Read the criteria for test and rules for exams carefully!

Digital exams are for project hand ins and other kind of hand ins and written examinations of all kind. Oral examinations do not use WISEflow.
